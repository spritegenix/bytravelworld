Magical Hong Kong Tour

  • PROGRAM

Program (5 Nights / 6 Days)

Experience the allure of Hong Kong with ByTravelYatra's 5 Nights/6 Days package. Dive into its vibrant energy, iconic landmarks, and cultural wonders. From bustling markets to panoramic views, every moment promises an unforgettable adventure.

  • Day 1

    Welcome to Hong Kong, on arrival you will be greeted our local representative. Sit in the coach and proceed to the Hotel and check in. After check in proceed to Hong Kong Night Tour (Peak Tram + Madame Tussauds + Harbour Cruise) sightseeing tour. Witness the sparkle of Hong Kong on this exciting city tour at night! First, hop on the iconic Peak Tram and enjoy a ride to Victoria Peak, the highest mountain in Hong Kong. Soak in the fabulous views of the city, as the tram chugs through the lush greens. Next, head to the Madame Tussauds Museum and get clicked with your favourite celebrities. Then, continue to the harbour cruise. Board the Star Ferry and set sail across the sparkling waters, gliding past iconic sites of Hong Kong. Your day comes to an end with a visit to the vibrant night market. You might also get to watch the Symphony of Lights Show during the tour. After complete sightseeing return to hotel.

    Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 2

    Enjoy a delicious breakfast at the hotel. Get ready to experience Hong Kong by proceeding on a Half Day City Tour. Discover Hong Kong distinctive fusion of contemporary and historic sites. First, board the famous Peak Tram and travel to Victoria Peak, Hong Kong highest mountain. Enjoy the breathtaking metropolitan views as the tram rumbles through the verdant landscape. After that, visit the Madame Tussauds Museum to take photos with your favourite stars. proceed to the harbour cruise after that. Take the Star Ferry and travel across the glistening seas while passing by famous Hong Kong landmarks. Visit the thriving night market to cap off your busy day.

    Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 3

    Have a scrumptious breakfast at the hotel. Enjoy a day of fun with your loved ones at Ocean Park, the second-largest theme park in Hong Kong. Have fun exploring the various exhibitions, seeing marine and land animals, and experiencing the exhilarating rides at the amusement park. This trip ensures a great experience because it has activities for all age groups.

  • Day 4

    Wake up to a beautiful day and get ready to head on a tour to explore the beautiful Lantau Island. The island offers a variety of activities and is home to stunning beaches, hiking trails, charming villages, and the famous Tian Tan Buddha Monument. Take a thrilling journey on the well-known Ngong Ping 360 Cable Car to start. As you travel to the bustling Ngong Ping Village, take in the breathtaking views of the surrounding environment. A trip to Po Lin Monastery puts an interesting day to a close.Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 5

    The day is at leisure for you to enjoy the city on your own. A full-day trip to Disneyland (Optional Tour, additional supplement is applicable) will make your holiday even more magical! Meet beloved Disney characters, ride iconic rides, and explore the theme park. In addition, you can enjoy spectacular musicals and shows that are performed by an extraordinary group and are guaranteed to make you smile. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 6

    Wake up in the morning, have a scrumptious breakfast and pack your bags for your departure. Complete the check-out formalities and get ready to leave to the airport to catch a suitable flight back home. Reach your desired destination and end the trip with unforgettable memories.
