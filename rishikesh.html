Divine Rishikesh and Haridwar

  • DESCRIPTION
  • PROGRAM

Haridwar and Rishikesh, two enchanting cities in the Indian state of Uttarakhand, are nestled along the sacred Ganges River and hold immense spiritual significance. Haridwar, often referred to as the "Gateway to God," is one of Hinduism's holiest places. Pilgrims flock to its ghats, especially Har Ki Pauri, to cleanse their sins in the sacred waters. The city buzzes with religious fervor, hosting the grand Kumbh Mela periodically. Rishikesh, just upstream, is renowned as the "Yoga Capital of the World." Its serene ambiance and numerous ashrams attract yoga enthusiasts and seekers of inner peace. The iconic Lakshman Jhula and Ram Jhula bridges span the river, connecting devotees to ancient temples and the tranquil Himalayan foothills. Both cities epitomize spirituality and offer a unique blend of divinity and natural beauty.

Program ( 4 Nights / 3 Days )

  • Day 1

    On arrival at Delhi airport, meet our representatives who’ll arrange a smooth transfer for you to Rishikesh. Check in to the hotel at Rishikesh. Fresh end up & get ready for water Rafting experience. After complete Rafting return to hotel. Overnight stay at hotel

  • Day 2

    Morning breakfast at hotel. Get ready for Rishikesh Sightseeing. covering Ram Jhula, Lakshman Jhula, Bharat Mandir, Triveni Ghat, Shivananda Ashram and Parmarth Ashram. After complete sightseeing return to hotel. Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 3

    Morning breakfast at hotel. Transfer from Rishikesh to Haridwar. Check in to the hotel. Once you check in to your hotel we shall proceed to participate in the famous Ganga Aarti. Here you will see one of the most magnetic sights of your lives. Let your mind be one with the divine as saffron-clad pujaris swing the massive aarti with fervor and send the earthen diyas floating in the holy river. Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 4

    Morning breakfast at hotel. Transfer from Haridwar to New Delhi
