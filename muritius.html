While travelling you can explore La Place D’Armes, Grand Bay, Roches Noires Caves, ILe aux Cerfs, Pamplemousses, Rochester Falls and Flic en Flac to capture amazing moments.

Program ( 6 Nights / 7 Days )

Immerse yourself in the beauty of Mauritius with ByTravelYatra's 6 Nights/7 Days package. From the moment you arrive, you'll be greeted by the allure of the Indian Ocean's turquoise waters and pristine beaches. Explore the captivating Ile Aux Cerfs Island with its stunning beaches and clear waters, or simply unwind on the beach, engaging in water activities at your leisure.

  • Day 1

    On Arrival Transfer to your Hotel.

    Surrounded by the Indian Ocean on the southeast coast of Africa is Mauritius. Lagoons, coral reefs, and clean beaches are enough to attract the attention of many vacationers, but it’s the pockets of history and gifts of nature within the mountains that enthral the keen adventurer.

    Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 2

    Trou d’Eau Douce

    After Breakfast. Drive to Trou d’Eau Douce - Ile Aux Cerfs Island. It is the islet with the most beautiful beach and the clearest turquoise water. Free on island up to 15h30hrs. Return back to mainland by speedboat.

    Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 3

    Relax on beach

    After Breakfast, you can indulge in beach walk or kayaking or glass bottom boat ride (under own arrangement).After Breakfast, you can indulge in beach walk or kayaking or glass bottom boat ride (under own arrangement).

    Dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 4

    North Islands Tour

    After Breakfast, Proceed for a full-day tour of the North Islands. We will be visiting the capital city, Port Louis, to enjoy its typical French colonial buildings. For some quick adrenalin rush we would now dash for Champ de Mars Racecourse. Then there are the exotic Caudan Waterfront to stop and shop to your heart’s content. Additionally, you may visit Pamplemousses Garden (Optional), the National Botanical Garden and Grand Baie (Optional). Come back to the hotel for dinner and stay at the hotel.

  • Day 5

    Riviere Noire Tour

    After Breakfast, enjoy a combo tour in one hassle-free day in Mauritius. After a convenient pickup from your hotel, ride a speedboat to Riviere Noire and swim with dolphins in the open sea using provided snorkel gear. There’s the possibility of spotting whales, too. Then, at Casela World of Adventures, a nature park, you’ll have plenty of time to independently explore or participate in many activities (own expense) before drop-off back to your hotel. (Under Own Arrangement).

  • Day 6

    South Island Tour

    After breakfast, proceed for a full-day tour of the South Island of Mauritius. Visit Curepipe, a model ship factory/Diamond Factory shop, Trou Aux Cerfs the crater of an extinct volcano Proceed for Grand Bassin- Sacred Lake for Hindus in Mauritius and Shiva Temple. Drive through Plaine Champagne & Black River Gorges and visit the 23-coloured Nature Park for a surreal experience. At the end of the day, come back to the hotel, have dinner and retire for the night.

  • Day 7

    Departure

    After breakfast, transfer to airport to take a flight back to India with wonderful memories.
