Alluring Thailand

  • DESCRIPTION
  • PROGRAM

Thailand attracts its visitors for the tropical beauty, scrumptious delicacies and the never dull nightlife. The place is chaotic but charming that stays with its visitors asking them to travel every now and then. The Alcazar shows a world-famous cabaret, which will transport you to the Lido and Moulin Rouge feels, the theatre features 1200 seats with pulsating sound. The place in the heart of mainland Southeast Asia should surely be on your next travel list! Think no more, and book with EaseMyTrip for hassle free booking experience.

  • - Breakfast and dinner included
  • - Health care included
  • - Transfer to the airport and return to the agency

Program ( 2 Nights / 3 Days )

  • Day 1

    Arrival in Bangkok - Upon arrival at the airport, meet the representative who would help you to assist to reach transfer to the Pattaya. 150Km Southeast of Bangkok, Pattaya is approx. 90 mins by road, which is Thailand's largest resort city. In the evening, we recommend that you take an optional tour of Alcazar Show (at additional cost). The Alcazar shows a world-famous cabaret, which can the feel of the Lido & Moulin Rouge of Paris, a run for their money, featuring a grand theatre with 1200 seats and thousands of watts of pulsating sound. Standard Check In time is 1400 Hours (Early Check-in is subject to availability) .Overnight stay in at Pattaya hotel.

  • Day 2

    Have Breakfast at the comfort of the hotel. After breakfast proceed to Half Day Coral Island Tour. you will be picked up and head to Pattaya Beach where the speedboat takes you over to the beautiful Koh Larn Coral Island. As you arrive, be amazed by its vast white-sand beaches and crystal-clear blue water. Plan your stay how you like it—either relax on the beach, snooze in the sun, swim in the ocean, or try out some water sports, including water skiing, parasailing, and windsurfing (for an additional fee and subject to availability). After an action-packed or relaxed day, the speedboat brings you back to Pattaya Beach, where your shuttle bus returns you to your hotel. Travel tip: Don’t forget to carry your swimsuit, sunscreen and towel. Overnight stay in Hotel.

  • Day 3

    Transfer from Pattaya to Bangkok Today after breakfast, check-out from your hotel and proceed for the Bangkok. In route half day city & Temple Tour of Bangkok. After complete Sightseeing process to hotel . Check in .Overnight stay in hotel.

  • Day 3

    Today after breakfast, Day is free at leisure at your own today you can shop at nearest market and can enjoy locality or you can opt for any optional tours to enjoy. Overnight at the hotel.

  • Day 3

    Early morning enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Later check out from the hotel, you will be transferred to Bangkok International airport to catch your flight to fly back to India. Bid goodbye to Thailand with Unlimited Happy Memories!
