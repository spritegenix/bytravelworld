Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

ByTravelWorld.com is an aggregator platform that connects users with travel service providers, including airlines, hotels, and tour operators. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, billing address, and travel preferences.
  • Payment Information: Securely processed through third-party payment gateways.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and website interactions for analytics and security.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Used for website optimization and user experience improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitate travel bookings and transactions.
  • Improve user experience and offer personalized recommendations.
  • Process payments securely through third-party providers.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Third-Party Sharing

  • Airlines, hotels, and tour operators to process bookings.
  • Payment gateways for secure transactions.
  • Legal authorities if required.

4. Data Security & User Rights

  • SSL encryption ensures data protection.
  • Users can access, update, or request deletion of their data.

For privacy concerns, contact info@bytravelworld.com.

Package Booking Policy

ByTravelWorld.com acts as an aggregator, connecting users with third-party travel service providers. The following terms apply:

1. Booking Process

  • Users can search, compare, and book packages from multiple travel operators.
  • Bookings are confirmed based on third-party availability.

2. Payments & Pricing

  • Prices are dynamic and may change based on provider policies.
  • Full or partial payment is required at the time of booking.

3. Cancellation & Modifications

  • Changes or cancellations must be made according to the provider’s policy.
  • ByTravelWorld.com is not responsible for refunds; these are processed by the provider.

4. Travel Documents & Responsibility

  • Users must have valid travel documents.
  • ByTravelWorld.com is not responsible for visa rejections or denied boarding.

5. Travel Insurance

  • We recommend purchasing travel insurance for cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost baggage.

For queries, contact info@bytravelworld.com.
