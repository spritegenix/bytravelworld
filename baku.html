A Journey through Baku and Absheron

  • DESCRIPTION
  • PROGRAM

Explore the vibrant charm of Azerbaijan with a captivating 4-day itinerary. Begin your journey in Baku, where you'll be greeted at the International Airport and whisked away to your hotel. Delve into the city's allure as you wander through its windy streets and soak in the contemporary architecture. The second day unfolds with a Baku urban excursion, featuring iconic landmarks like Highland Park, Flame Towers, and the Azerbaijan Carpet Museum. On day three, embark on an Absheron tour, discovering cultural gems such as Heydar Aliyev Center, Ateshgah Fire Temple, and the mesmerizing Yanar Dagh (Burning Mountain). Your adventure comes to an end on day 4, where you head to the Azerbaijan International Airport for your departure after having a hearty breakfast in the hotel.

Program ( 2 Nights / 3 Days )

  • Day 1

    Arrive at the Azerbaijan International Airport. You will be welcomed by the tour driver and Then transferred to your hotel.

    Check-in at the hotel.

    The rest of the day is free for you to explore this windy capital

    Overnight in Baku.

  • Day 2

    Breakfast at the hotel in Baku

    Excursion program (pick-up time 10:00):

    • Highland Park and Flame Towers Complex
    • Ferris Wheel International
    • Mugham and “Baku Venice”
    • Azerbaijan Carpet Museum
    • Baku Boulevard
    • Old city tour (Maiden Tower, Shirvanshah Palace, etc)
    • Fountain Square

    Transfer to Hotel

    Overnight in Baku.

  • Day 3

    Breakfast at the hotel.

    Excursion program (pick-up time 10:00):

    • Heydar Aliyev Center (outside)
    • Ateshgah – Fire temple
    • Yanar Dagh (Burning Mountain)

    Transfer to Hotel

    Overnight in Baku.

  • Day 4

    Breakfast at the hotel.

    Check out from Hotel

    Transfer to the Azerbaijan International Airport.

    Departure.
