Fantastic Bali Group Departure

  • DESCRIPTION
  • PROGRAM

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the enchanting island of Bali with our meticulously crafted Bali tour package. Begin your adventure in the vibrant city of Kuta, unwinding at the hotel or soaking up the sun at the nearest beach. Indulge in retail therapy at Discovery Mall and experience Bali's famed nightlife at Rock Bar. Discover the beauty of Kintamani Volcano and the artistic villages of Tohpati, Celuk, and Mas. Marvel at Mount & Lake Batur's stunning vistas and savor exotic coffees at a local plantation. Visit the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, perched atop a dramatic rock formation overlooking the Indian Ocean, and immerse yourself in Balinese culture. Experience adrenaline-pumping water sports at Tanjung Benoa beach, enjoying banana boat rides, jet skiing, and parasailing for an exhilarating day. Indulge in a variety of activities in Seminyak, from exploring Bali Safari and Marine Park to unwinding at Potato Head Beach Club or diving into vibrant nightlife.

Program ( 5 Nights / 6 Days )

  • Day 1

    Hello Bali! Welcome to Bali. Upon your arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport, meet our representative at the arrival hall who will escort you till your private car. Arrive at the hotel and check in. Kuta is where all the action takes place in Bali. Home to a plethora of nightclubs, street shops and the most popular beaches. Day is at leisure for you to explore on your own. You can unwind at the hotel or relax at the nearest beach. Visit the Discovery Mall and bring back truckloads of bling for yourself! The beauty of this mall lies not only in shopping but also in its scenic location. There are many cafes and restaurants where you can relax after you are done indulging in retail therapy. As the sun sets you must checkout the famous nightlife spots such as the Rock Bar at Ayana Resort, perched on cliffs above the Indian Ocean. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 2

    Full Day Kintamani Volcano Tour with Ubud Village Kickstart your morning with the scrumptious breakfast Visit Kintamani Volcano, a rare opportunity to experience one of Bali’s last remaining active volcanos up close.The tour begins with the sights of a vibrant art village. Meet with the locals who keep the techniques of the region’s handicrafts alive with a stop at Tohpati village, widely known as the centre of handmade Balinese Batik weaving. Next stop is Celuk village famous for producing high-grade gold and silver handicrafts. Continue trip to Mas Village, one of the many artists villages surrounding Ubud, is the best place on the island to find high-quality Balinese woodcarvings, considered some of the best woodcarvings in the world. The tour continues to the Mount & Lake Batur in Kintamani village for some stunning views of the beautiful locales in this region. End your tour in one of the most famous coffee plantations in the area. Here you can look & taste many kinds of coffees from Indonesia. Drive past the Tegalalang village, with some of the most beautiful views of rice terraces. Upon enjoying your fun-filled day, return back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Day 3

    Relax on beach

    Half Day Tanah Lot Temple Tour Wake up to a new day and refuel your energy with a hearty breakfast meal and head to Tanah Lot Temple, probably the most popular and photographed temple on the island. Tanah Lot Temple stands for Earth (Tanah) and Sea (Lot), quite appropriate considering its spectacular setting on top of an impressive rock overlooking the Indian Ocean. At the entrance lane to Tanah Lot Temple, you will find a beautiful architectural Balinese gate and many art shops offering local handicrafts besides paintings, woodcarvings, and more. Overnight stay at your hotel.

  • Day 4

    Head to Seminyak | Half Day Benoa Beach (Water Sports) Wake up with a delicious breakfast at the hotel. Add an adrenaline kick to your Bali vacation with a choice of thrilling water sports and activities at Tanjung Benoa beach. Located in the Benoa peninsula far away from the hustle & bustle enjoy a fun banana boat ride, zip along the beach on a jet ski or try exhilarating water sports like parasailing. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

  • Day 5

    Wake up in the morning, have a delicious breakfast at the hotel. Day is at leisure for you to explore on your own. You can head to Bali Safari and Marine Park (at extra cost). Bali’s most realistic safari experience takes you on a journey to discover the inhabitants of Indonesia, India and the African Savannah. Find elephants, tigers, leopards, kimodo dragons, cheetahs, and orangutans, to name a few. Watch the elephants bathing or even climb on for a ride, visit the petting zoo, learn a Balinese dance, see almost 40 species of fish, and don’t miss feeding time for the white tigers and piranhas! Else, enjoy the Panoramic View at Potato Head Beach Club, Relax and splurge in a typical Bali style spa treatment for your tired body with an assortment of relaxing treatments, Visit Iconic Pura Petitenget Temple, Party at Ku De Ta, Detox in a Yoga Class & many more. If you are a beach lover, a host of thrilling water activities to do in Seminyak like scuba diving and surfing await you here. On the other hand, if you are a night owl, you can spend the night partying at Bali Joe Bar or watching a movie under the open starry sky at Paradiso. If you are looking for unique activities in Seminyak, you might want to ditch the malls and nightclubs and head to the local markets. The night markets in Seminyak are home to several small stalls and shops where you can find a variety of local delights to shop for. Overnight stay at the hotel. (Please note that these are suggested activities, supplement charges will be applicable)

  • Day 6

    Wake up in the morning, have a scrumptious breakfast and pack your bags for your departure. Complete the check-out formalities and get ready to leave to the airport to catch a suitable flight to back home. Reach your desired destination and end the trip with unforgettable memories.
